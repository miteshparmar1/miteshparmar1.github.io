Our Story

RHP Estates' plantation, locally known as Bandarapola Lodge and has always operated under strong family values. Originally purchased by our maternal grandfather Anton Ratwatte as his retirement project in1968 it has now been in the family for three generations.

We continue to build on the existing plantation and are looking at expanding our operations to allow growth and provide even more opportunities to the local community with which we have a strong relationship and an active part to play.

By working with us, you choose to work with a modern and expanding Sri-Lankan enterprise that still maintains strong traditional family values.
